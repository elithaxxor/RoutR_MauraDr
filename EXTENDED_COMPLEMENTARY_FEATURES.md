# Extended Complementary Feature Proposal

To further complement RoutR_MauraDr, consider the following enhancements:

1. **Automated Cleanup of Temporary Files**
   - After scans and exports, automatically purge leftover temporary directories.
2. **Live Topology Annotations**
   - Allow administrators to place notes directly on nodes within the D3.js force graph.
3. **Baseline Policy Recommendations**
   - Suggest remediation steps when baseline deviations are discovered.
4. **Scheduled Certificate Audits**
   - Periodically re-check certificate expiry and notify via email or webhook.
5. **Modular Export Plugins**
   - Provide a plugin interface for exporting results to formats such as CSV or Markdown.
