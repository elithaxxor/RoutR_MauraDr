# Additional Proposed Enhancements

The following ideas could further extend RoutR_MauraDr:

1. **Browser-Based Quick Scan Extension**
   - Provide a lightweight browser extension to trigger scans directly from a web browser.
2. **Data Retention Policies**
   - Allow administrators to configure automatic pruning of old scan data.
3. **Virtualization Platform Integration**
   - Connect to common hypervisor APIs to enumerate guest networks and assets.
